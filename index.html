Common Questions

Everything you need to know about professional home sanitization.

How does UV-C light kill viruses and bacteria?

UV-C light works by destroying the molecular bonds in the DNA and RNA of microorganisms. This process prevents them from reproducing and renders them completely harmless, effectively "killing" them without chemicals.

Is it safe for smartphones and laptops?

Yes. Unlike chemical sprays that can cause moisture damage or corrosion, ClairLite™ uses only light. It is the safest way to sanitize sensitive electronic surfaces where bacteria often thrive.

Can I stay in the room during sanitization?

No. For safety, humans, pets, and plants should leave the room while the device is active. ClairLite™ includes a built-in remote control and delayed start timer so you can safely activate it from outside the room.

How long does the UV-C bulb last?

The high-grade quartz bulb is rated for over 8,000 hours of usage. For most households, this translates to years of daily sanitization without ever needing a replacement bulb.

Safety Notice: Do not expose eyes or skin to active UV-C light. Use only as directed in the provided manual. ClairLite™ is intended for surface sanitization.
